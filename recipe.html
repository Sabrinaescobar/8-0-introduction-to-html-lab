Oat Pancakes

Description

This is a healthy recipe, also is delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of oats
  • 1 cup of almond milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 table spoon of olive oil

Directions

  1. Blend the oats.
  2. In a bowl mix the oats, the almond milk, the egg and the olive oil.
  3. On a hot pan, put the mix and when it has some bubles turn it around.
  4. Serve them with fruit on them and honey.
  5. Your pancakes are ready to eat. Enjoy them!!!
